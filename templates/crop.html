Best Crops for Each Month in Gujarat

Month Crop Soil Type

Crop Recommendations

  • Cotton: Best suited for Black Soil, grows well in April and September.
  • Groundnut: Prefers Sandy Loam, ideal for sowing from April to June.
  • Rice: Grows in Clayey Soil during the monsoon (June).
